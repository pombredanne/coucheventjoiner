    Damien's international brunch

    Organized by Damien Birambeau
    • Flag this event

    Reason for flagging

    Cancel
    Citizens of the world in search of a moment of sharing in a friendly atmosphere, come join us !

    Everyone bring something, whether it's a specialty of your country or your favorite bakery pastries, the main thing is to participate!

    This event will take place at my apartment in nation district every two weeks. To have the exact address you just have to join and then I will contact you with the location information.

    5 reasons to join :
    - no need to bring cash, just bring food
    - Cup of tea, coffee, pain au chocolat will be served
    - getting to know new people in a friendly atmosphere (the music will not be so loud so no need to yell)
    - tasting new food elaborate by the participants
    - taking the time to discover new cultures

    Of course if you have any question and suggestion just contact me.
    (If you have organisational skills, don't hesitate to bring your contribution to make this a unique moment)
